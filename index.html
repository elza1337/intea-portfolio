How we work

The MIDAS framework.

Every project we deliver is shaped by our own instructional design framework — built over 17 years to make training stick.

Most organisations are good at informing, developing, and assessing. The parts that get skipped — motivation and support — are the ones that make the difference. MIDAS keeps all five in play.

Tell us about your project →
M

Motivate

Engage learners before the content even starts. Don't assume motivation.

I

Inform

Deliver only what's relevant to the learning goal. No more, no less.

D

Develop

Build real skills through practice, exercise, and applied tasks.

A

Assess

Measure progress with meaningful, fair testing that respects the learner.

S

Support

Reinforce knowledge long after the course ends. Sandwich your training.
